Revenue = directRevenue (business ops in) + projectProfit (sum of projects.profit) + containerProfit (sum of containers.profit)
Expenses = businessExpenses (business ops out including asset purchases)
Net Profit = Revenue - Expenses

Asset purchase: full value registered as debit (out) to vendor contact. Payments (full or installment) registered as credit (in) to vendor.
